Intresting Facts About Camel

  • Camels are social animals who roam the deserts in search of food and water with up to 30 other individuals.
  • With the exception of rutting males competing for females, camels are very peaceful animals who rarely exhibit aggression.
  • Contrary to popular misconception, camels do not store water in their humps. The humps are actually reservoirs for fatty tissue.
  • Asian camels have two humps whereas Arabian camels only have one.

    • Intresting Facts About Cicada

  • Cicadas are the elders of the insect world.
  • New groups of young are born every year, so every year different generations emerge.
  • Female cicadas lay from 200 to 600 eggs in tiny holes made in branches and twigs in trees and shrubs.
  • There are two groups of cicadas: dog-day cicadas and periodical cicadas.
